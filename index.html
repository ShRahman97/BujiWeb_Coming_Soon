Questions? We love them.

Here are some of the things people ask before booking with us.

Is Buji safe?

We rigorously vet every clinic and surgeon we work with. Only partners that meet our safety, accreditation and outcomes criteria make it onto our platform.

How long do I need to travel for?

Most hair transplant patients stay 3–4 nights. That includes your consultation, procedure, first wash and an in-person post-op check before flying home.

What’s included in the price?

Your package typically covers the procedure, anaesthesia, medications, airport transfers, hotel, translation and in-person aftercare. Full details are in our “What’s included” breakdown.

What happens if something goes wrong?

You’ll have direct access to both your surgeon and our UK team. We’ll coordinate any follow-up care you need and support you until things are resolved.
