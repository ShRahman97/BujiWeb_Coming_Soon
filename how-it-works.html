How it works

Your path to restoration starts here. Follow these five steps to begin your journey with us, and we'll be there every step of the way.

1

Share three angles

Upload three key, clinical photos (front, top, and side) of your hair loss via our secure app. Our clinical team will use these to provide an initial assessment.

2

GP review

A UK-registered GP will review your case and provide a diagnosis and treatment plan. This will be shared with you via our secure app.

3

Approve & unlock concierge

The treatment plan will be sent to you for approval. Once approved, you'll unlock your dedicated concierge, who will handle all travel arrangements.

4

Fly In. Check In. Restore.

Travel to your chosen clinic, check into your accommodation, and settle in. Our team will be on hand to ensure a smooth and comfortable experience.

5

Grow with us

Your new hair will begin to grow in 12 weeks. Our GP and aftercare team will provide ongoing support.
